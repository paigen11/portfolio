Hi, I'm Paige.

I'm a full stack web developer with a background in digital marketing.

Marketing is great and something I enjoy, but I really want to be able to build the ideas I come up with and have a career that keeps me learning and solving problems every day. That's why I decided to pursue web development.

About

In July of 2016, I began a rigorous, full-time coding bootcamp with DigitalCrafts in Atlanta, GA, with the end goal of being a full stack web developer.

The pace was intense, but in that short time I've been trained in a wide variety of programming languages. My front end experience includes HTML5, CSS3 (both native and SASS), JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX and AngularJS. My back end experience is in Node.js, Express, MongoDB, Mongoose, Python, MySQL and Flask. I also use Git on an almost daily basis. In addition to these languages, I've been taught to think like a developer through daily problem solving, algorithm and coding challenges.

And I love it. Bringing ideas to life, learning and applying something new, and finally figuring out the reason a piece of code wasn't working is the most satisfying feeling. I want to work with smart people on great projects every day and make ideas into reality.

When I'm not coding, you can find me with my husband watching movies, reading a good book, cooking and baking, and planning where to travel to next.
